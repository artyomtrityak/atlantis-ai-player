Production comes AFTER tax maximization. TAX = $50/man/turn vs production = $24-26/item for basic goods. TAX has ~1.5 turn ROI; production has 4-6 turn ROI.

**Why:** TAX is month-long in NewOrigins (TAX_PILLAGE_MONTH_LONG = 1), so a unit CANNOT both TAX and PRODUCE. Dedicated production units earn less per man than taxers. Only invest in production after all tax regions are maxed.

**How to apply:**
1. SELL (phase 14) runs before PRODUCE (month-long) → a unit can @SELL last month's output + PRODUCE new items in the same turn. Use this perpetual cycle.
2. Only cities/towns with "Wanted" lists accept SELL orders. Check where goods can be sold before producing.
3. Production units need skill in Farming/Mining/Lumberjack/etc. — NOT COMB. They are dedicated non-combat units.
4. Region "Products: N item" = monthly production cap for that region across all producers.
5. At skill level L, each man produces L items/month (formula: men × level × productivity / 10).
6. Best production chain: iron → swords (Mining + Weaponsmith). $90/sword at Rimouth. ~1.5 turn ROI for combined chain.
7. Simplest chain: livestock at Rimouth (Farming 1, produce + sell locally, no transport). $320 net/month.
8. Transport is expensive: Quartermaster costs $5/weight, needs Caravanserai. Manual transport halves effective revenue (2-turn MOVE cycle). Prefer producing at or adjacent to the selling city.
