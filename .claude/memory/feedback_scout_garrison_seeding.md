Scouts should always drop new garrison units (FORM + BUY men + STUDY COMB) at every viable region they pass through, not just explore passively.

**Why:** Tax garrisons cost ~$300-500 to set up but return $200-600/turn. The ROI is 1-2 turns. Scouts that only explore waste the opportunity to grow the empire's economy every turn.

**How to apply:** When writing orders for any scout unit entering a new region:
1. Check if the region has men for sale and decent economy ($500+)
2. FORM a new Praesidium unit, CLAIM silver, GIVE to it
3. New unit: BUY local men (5+), STUDY COMB, TURN block with @GUARD 1 + @TAX
4. Scout continues MOVE to next region
