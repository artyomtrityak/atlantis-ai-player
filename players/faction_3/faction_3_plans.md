## Tax maximization rule

**NEVER BUY men into an existing COMB-trained unit.** This dilutes combat skill below level 1, making the entire unit unable to tax. Always FORM a separate unit, have it BUY men and STUDY COMB for one turn, then TAX.

Formula: Men needed to max region = ceil(region_economy / 50).

## Phase 3: Production Economy (starts turn 8-9)

### Why production comes AFTER tax maximization
- TAX = $50/man/turn (ROI ~1.5 turns). Production = $24-26/item (ROI ~4-6 turns).
- TAX is month-long in NewOrigins — a unit CANNOT both TAX and PRODUCE.
- Always max tax in every region first. Production is for additional income on top.

### Key rules
- SELL (phase 14) runs BEFORE PRODUCE (month-long) → a unit can @SELL last month's output + PRODUCE more in the same turn. Perpetual cycle.
- Only Rimouth has "Wanted" items → all goods must be sold at Rimouth.
- Production units need the relevant skill (Farming, Mining, etc.) — NOT COMB. They are dedicated non-combat producers.
- Region "Products: N item" = max N items can be produced per month in that region by all producers combined.
- At skill level L, each man produces L items/month (capped by region limit).
