# Faction 4 behavior rules

This is Faction 4 behavior rules explaining playstyle of the Faction 4.

Follow those rules when making turns, for example if the faction is aggressive towards others, or prefers peace; likes to play trade faction or magic; etc.

## Main playstyle

The Faction 4 likes to learn magic, find and settle in the safe place, settlement, train mages and learn string magic, hunt monsters once they are powerful enough.
